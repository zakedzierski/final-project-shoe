Why Make This Website?

I will now inform you what this whole website is about. We are a group of girls who participated in the GIRLS WHO CODE SUMMER IMMERSION PROGRAM in Chicago, IL. We were given the opportunity to create any final project we wanted and this is what we cam up with. We wanted to solve a problem. All the girls in our group are highschool cross country runners and brought up the problem of overused running shoes causing injury. This website should be able to, with the help of user input, remind you to buy new running shoes when your already used shoes have reached their maximum mileage.
